✅ Template Features:

  • 🔒 FROZEN HEADERS: Headers stay visible when scrolling!
  • 📊 GRAND TOTAL: See total consumption at bottom-right

🔢 How Totals Work:

  • Just type numbers in the reagent columns
  • Total column: Updates automatically (sum of all reagents for that customer)
  • % column: Shows customer's percentage of grand total
  • TOTAL row: Shows total for each reagent across all customers
  • Grand Total: Bottom-right cell = sum of everything

📝 Excel Formula Cells:

  • Column "Total" (right side) = =SUM(all reagents)
  • Column "%" = =Total/GrandTotal
  • Row "TOTAL" (bottom) = =SUM(all customers)
