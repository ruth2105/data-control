════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  WHEN DO YOU SEE WHICH LAYOUT?
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TWO PANELS (Left + Right):
✅ Visit report has company product data
✅ Any of these fields filled: otherBrand1, wondfostockName,
   elecBrand, bioBrand, hemBrand, tubeBrand, btBrand, needleBrand

ONE PANEL (Right only):
✅ Visit report has NO company product data
✅ None of the product fields filled
✅ Summary still shows at bottom!


════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  SUMMARY SECTION (ALWAYS SHOWS IF EXISTS)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The "📝 Hospital Summary" section appears at the BOTTOM of the 
right panel in BOTH scenarios:

✅ TWO PANELS: Shows at bottom of right panel
✅ ONE PANEL: Shows at bottom of single panel

The summary is IMPORTED from CSV or WRITTEN by sales people when
they create new visit reports in "Discussion Notes" field.


════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  HOW TO GET TWO-PANEL VIEW
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

If you want to see TWO PANELS for a hospital:

1. Edit the visit report
2. Fill in company product data:
   
   OPTION 1 - Wondfo Products:
   • Other Brand 1: ACCRE A8
   • Stock Name: Hormone reagents
   • Stock Qty: 50 boxes
   
   OPTION 2 - Competitor Equipment:
   • Electrolyte Brand: B&E
   • Bio Brand: Mindray
   • Hem Brand: Sysmex
   
   OPTION 3 - Consumables:
   • Tube Brand: Vacutainer
   • BT Brand: [brand]
   • Needle Brand: [brand]

3. Save the report
4. Reopen the report detail
5. You'll see TWO PANELS!


════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  QUICK REFERENCE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LEFT PANEL = 🏭 Our Company Products
─────────────────────────────────────
Shows: What they use from OUR company
Displays: ACCRE machines, reagents, competitor comparison
Condition: Only if product data exists


RIGHT PANEL = 🏥 Hospital Info
──────────────────────────────
Shows: Hospital details + Visit info + Summary
Displays: All basic info, contacts, progress, summary
Condition: Always shows


SUMMARY SECTION = 📝 Hospital Summary
─────────────────────────────────────
Shows: Detailed summary (imported or written)
Location: Bottom of right panel
Condition: Shows if notes field has data
