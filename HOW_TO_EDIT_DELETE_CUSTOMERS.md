## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Can I edit other salespeople's customers?
**A:** No, you can only edit your own customers. Admin can edit all.

### Q: Will my edits sync across devices?
**A:** Currently saves to browser localStorage. For cross-device sync, submit a visit report for that customer.

### Q: Can I undo a delete?
**A:** No, deletion is permanent. Be careful! Confirm you're deleting the right customer.

### Q: What happens if I delete a customer?
**A:** They're removed from all pages (Visit Reports, Customer Potential, Dashboard). Visit reports for that customer are NOT deleted.

### Q: Can I add new customers?
**A:** Yes! Submit a visit report form. New customers automatically appear with ✨ NEW badge.

### Q: How do I know which customers I can edit?
**A:** You'll see ✏️ and 🗑️ buttons only on customers assigned to you.

### Q: What if I make a mistake?
**A:** Just edit again and fix it. Admin can also help correct mistakes.
