## What Sales Can Do

### ✅ Add Visit Reports
- Open `app/sales.html`
- Click "📝 New Visit Report"
- Fill form:
  - Hospital name, address, contact
  - Equipment information
  - Customer potential (Hot/Warm/Cold)
  - Visit progress and notes
- Click "Save"
- Report saved to system!

### ✅ View Their Customers
- Dashboard with statistics
- Filter by Hot/Warm/Cold
- Search by hospital
- Edit existing reports

### ✅ Announce Purchases
- When customer agrees to buy
- Click "Announce Purchase" button
- Admin gets notified
